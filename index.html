How to Make a Thick Chocolate Chip Walnut Cookie

by Phoebe Yaheng Wu

If you know me, you know, I'm a BIG chocolate cookie lover, who has to eat a giant cookie every day. At $5 a cookie, this is a costly habit, which has driven me to learn how to make BIG chocolate cookies myself. My favorite is the thick Chocolate Chip Walnut Cookie that has a crispy outside, and soft and chewy inside. Below, I'm sharing a recipe for a Chocolate Chip Walnut Cookie, which is as good as the $5 cookies. If you follow along with each step, you too will have a delicious cookie.

Below is the picture of my first attempt at making a Chocolate Chip Walnut Cookie. Yes, it does taste as good as it looks!


Below are all the Ingredients you need:
  • 200g Unsalted Butter - soften, not melted
  • 125g Brown Sugar
  • 75g Sugar
  • 2.5g Salt
  • 2 Eggs
  • 7g Vanilla Extract
  • 320g All Purpose Flour
  • 3g Baking Powder
  • 2g Baking Soda
  • 150g Walnuts and Pecans (cut into small pieces)
  • 100g Dark Chocolate (cut into small pieces)
  • 50g Dark Chocolate Chips
Below are the steps you can follow along:
Step 1:

Place walnuts and pecans on a baking tray
Place All Purpose Flour on a separate baking tray
Put the two baking trays in a preheated oven at 180C/356F for 10 min on top/bottom heating mode
Take out the flour, and stir it lightly to prevent clumps

Step 2:

Stir unsalted butter, make sure the butter is soft, not melted

Step 3:

Mix the following ingredients with the butter until they are blended. (This can be done by hand. No need to whip.)
Brown Sugar, Sugar, Salt

Step 4:

Add Eggs, one at a time, to the mixture

Step 5:

Add Vanilla Extract (about 2 bottle caps) and mix

Step 6:

Add the following ingredients and mix:
All Purpose Flour, Baking Powder, Baking Soda

Step 7:

Add the remaining ingredients and mix
Walnuts and Pecans, Dark Chocolate, Dark Chocolate Chips

Step 8:

Chill dough in the fridge for 30 mins

Step 9:

Scoop dough into a tall round ball

Step 10:

Put the rounded ball on a baking tray and place it inside the preheated Oven at 190C/374F for 15 mins on top/bottom heating mode

Step 11:

Remove from the oven and let it cool for 10 mins

Step 12:

Enjoy!😄
